The 40 Best Contractor Websites on The Web

The Contractor Website Designs industries have been around for years. It is a very competitive field, and contractor web designers have had to keep up with the latest trends to stay ahead of their competition. Construction companies need to be able to convert potential customers into paying clients. Most contractors’ businesses thrive by having a proper website that shows off their work and contact information that makes it easy for new clients to get in touch. In this blog post, we will be looking at 40 of the best contractor websites on the web. They are sure to provide you with some good inspiration.

1. House of Bohn

Why it works: The sleek design of House of Bohn evokes luxury, elegance, and class that entice visitors to explore more. The website has a modern layout with an easy-to-use navigation bar that sits at the top of the page. The company web page allows more customers to learn about their services, browse their portfolio, and quickly contact them.

houseofbohn.com

2. Delta Millworks

Why it works: This contractor website design has a dark-themed design and effectively uses gold as a color accent. The video background and high-quality images are aesthetically pleasing.

deltamillworks.com

3. Square Footage

Why it works: Designed like a portfolio website. Square Footage’s web design is both elegant and straightforward. The web designer made a simple layout with a focus on images and videos of their completed work. This makes it easy for website visitors to quickly get an idea of the construction company’s work and what they are capable of.

squarefootageinc.com
