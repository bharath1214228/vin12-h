The Top 40
Best Events Websites

Event websites are a hot topic in the world of business marketing right now, and for good reason. Event websites can help you generate leads and create a buzz in the world before your event even takes place! Event website design styles have changed drastically over the past few years, with many companies opting to go live and showcase on social media sites like Facebook instead.

Event planners need to know which style is best for the community to get started. In this blog post, we will take a look at 40 different best event websites that range from simple and sleek to flashy and creative!

1. Eventbrite

Why it works: This event website is clean, modern, beautiful, and has a flavorful aesthetic despite the minimalist appearance. The generous use of white space provided the site with an extra professional look.

eventbrite.com

2. SFTravel

Why it works: A very vibrant event site with strong colors, the right choice of typefaces, and images that pique interest and engagement abound.

sftravel.com

3. Shiloh Events

Why it works: The Shiloh Events website has a modern and elegant aesthetic with clear branding. The clean design of this website is easy to navigate or browse and provides all the information for visitors.

shiloh-events.com

4. Live Worx

Why it works: Live Worx’s website, on the other hand, is quite different. This dark yet elegant event site has a subtle moving hero background. The website also uses ultra-modern animations and other features, making it an excellent choice for this list.

liveworx.com
